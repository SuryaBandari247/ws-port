What is WithSwag?

WithSwag is a collection of free, browser-based tools built for creators, travellers, and everyday users. We believe useful tools should be accessible to everyone — no signups, no downloads, no hidden fees.

Every tool on WithSwag runs entirely in your browser. Your files, photos, and data never leave your device unless you choose to download the result. Privacy isn't a feature we bolt on — it's how we built everything from the ground up.

Our principle: Your data stays on your device. We don't upload, store, or process your files on any server. All processing happens locally in your browser using modern web technologies.

What We Offer

📸 Passport Photo Maker

Create compliant passport and visa photos for 50+ countries. AI background removal, automatic cropping to official dimensions, and print-ready collage sheets at 300 DPI.

📖 Photo Requirement Guides

Detailed guides for passport photos, visa photos, driving licence photos, and ID photos. Country-specific requirements in English, German, Spanish, Italian, French, and Dutch.

Why We Built This

We got tired of "free" tools that require accounts, upload your files to unknown servers, plaster watermarks on everything, or bait-and-switch you into paid plans. Simple tasks like resizing a passport photo shouldn't require installing software or handing over your email.

WithSwag is the alternative. Open the tool, do your thing, download the result. That's it.

How It Works

Browser-Based Processing

All our tools use client-side JavaScript, WebAssembly, and modern browser APIs. When you upload a photo, it's processed right there in your browser tab — nothing gets sent to a server.

Country-Specific Compliance

Our passport photo tool includes specifications for over 50 countries, sourced from official government documentation. We regularly update these specs to reflect the latest requirements from agencies like the US State Department, UK HMPO, German BMI, and others.

Free Guides

We publish detailed guides covering passport photo requirements by country, visa photo specs, and how-to tutorials. These guides are free, regularly updated, and written to help you get things right the first time.

Technology

WithSwag is built with modern web technologies:

  • React and TypeScript for the passport photo tool
  • AI-powered background removal using on-device machine learning
  • Hosted on Vercel's global CDN for fast loading worldwide
  • No backend servers — everything runs client-side
