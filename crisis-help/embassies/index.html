Register with Your Government

Registering helps your government locate and assist you during emergencies.

🇺🇸

STEP (US Citizens)

Smart Traveler Enrollment Program. Register your trip so the US Embassy can contact you in an emergency and help family/friends reach you.

Register at step.state.gov →
🇬🇧

FCDO (UK Citizens)

Register with the Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office for travel alerts and emergency assistance.

Register at gov.uk →
🇨🇦

ROCA (Canadian Citizens)

Registration of Canadians Abroad. Allows the government to contact you and provide assistance during emergencies.

Register at travel.gc.ca →
🇦🇺

Smartraveller (Australian Citizens)

Register your travel plans so the Australian government can assist you in a crisis.

Register at smartraveller.gov.au →
