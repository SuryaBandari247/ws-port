Key Australian Requirements

Australia follows the standard 35×45mm format used across much of the world. However, there are some specific rules:

  • No glasses — Australia does not allow glasses in passport photos, even prescription ones
  • Both ears visible — Hair must not cover your ears
  • No head coverings — Unless for religious or medical reasons
  • Neutral expression — No smiling, mouth must be closed
  • Eyes open and clearly visible — No squinting

Digital Photo for Online Applications

If applying online through the Australian Passport Office, your digital photo must be a high-quality JPEG file. The face must be centered and the background must be uniformly white.

Australian Visa Photo Requirements

Visa photos for Australia (tourist, student, work visas) use the same 35×45mm format. The requirements are identical to passport photos.
