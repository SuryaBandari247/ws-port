    Detailed Requirements

    Face and Expression

    Switzerland follows ICAO biometric standards. Your face must be centered and occupy 70–80% of the photo. Expression must be completely neutral with mouth closed. Both eyes must be open and looking directly at the camera. The face must be evenly illuminated without shadows.

    Background

    Switzerland requires a uniform light grey background. Pure white is not accepted. The background must be completely even — no gradients, shadows, or patterns. This is consistent with most other European countries.

    Tip: Swiss passport applications are handled at the Einwohnerkontrolle (residents' registration office) in your Gemeinde. They check photos carefully. WithSwag applies the exact light grey background that Swiss authorities require.

    Glasses Policy

    While Switzerland hasn't formally banned glasses, they are strongly discouraged. If you choose to wear them, there must be absolutely no glare on the lenses, no tinted lenses, and both eyes must be fully visible. The safest approach is to remove glasses entirely.

    Head Coverings

    Head coverings are not permitted unless worn for religious reasons. The face must be fully visible from chin to forehead, and both sides of the face must be clearly visible.

    Schengen Association

    Although Switzerland is not an EU member, it is part of the Schengen Area. Swiss passport photos follow the same ICAO biometric standards used across Schengen countries, making them compatible with EU travel requirements.

    What Documents Use This Photo?

    • Schweizer Pass / Passeport suisse — Swiss passport
    • Identitätskarte (ID-Karte) — National ID card
    • Aufenthaltsbewilligung — Residence permit
    • Schengen-Visum — Schengen visa applications through Switzerland
    • Führerausweis — Driving license

    Common Rejection Reasons

    1. White background — Must be light grey
    2. Glare on glasses — Remove glasses to be safe
    3. Shadows on face — Lighting must be completely even
    4. Face too small or too large — Must be 30–36mm chin to crown
    5. Expression not neutral — No smile, mouth closed
    6. Head tilted or turned — Must face camera directly
    7. Photo too old — Must be within 6 months
    8. Low resolution — Must be sharp and clear
