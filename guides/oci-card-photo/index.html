    OCI Card Photo Specifications

    SpecificationRequirement
    Photo size (print)2 × 2 inches (51 × 51 mm)
    Face coverage70–80% of the frame
    BackgroundPlain white
    ExpressionNeutral, mouth closed
    GlassesRecommended to remove
    EarsMust be visible
    RecencyWithin the last 6 months
    Print finishMatte or glossy accepted

    Digital Photo Requirements (Online Application)

    For online OCI applications through the OCI Services portal:

    SpecificationRequirement
    FormatJPEG / JPG
    File size10 KB – 300 KB
    Minimum dimensions350 × 350 pixels
    BackgroundPlain white
    Face coverage70–80% of frame
    Tip: OCI card photos follow the same specifications as Indian passport photos. If you have a recent Indian passport photo, you can use it for your OCI application.

    OCI vs. Indian Passport Photo — Any Difference?

    No. The photo requirements are identical:

    • Same size: 2×2 inches (51×51mm)
    • Same background: plain white
    • Same face coverage: 70–80%
    • Same digital specs for online applications

    The only difference is the application form and process — the photo itself is the same standard.

    Common Rejection Reasons

    1. Wrong background — Must be plain white, not off-white or coloured
    2. Face too small — Must cover 70–80% of the frame
    3. Ears not visible — Hair must not cover ears
    4. Shadows — No shadows on face or background
    5. File size wrong — Must be 10–300 KB for online applications
    6. Wrong dimensions — Must be at least 350×350 pixels digitally
    7. Photo too old — Must be within 6 months

    Where to Apply for OCI

    • Online: ociservices.gov.in
    • In person: Indian Embassy or Consulate in your country
    • Through VFS Global: In countries where VFS handles Indian visa services
