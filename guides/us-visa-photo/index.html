    DS-160 Photo Upload — Step by Step

    1. Take your photo following the requirements above
    2. Upload to WithSwag and select "US Passport/Visa" size

    4. On the DS-160 form, click "Upload Photo"
    5. Select your file — the system will verify dimensions automatically
    6. If it passes, you'll see a green checkmark
    Common DS-160 Upload Error: "Photo does not meet requirements." This usually means the file is too small (under 600×600px), too large (over 10MB), or not in JPEG format. WithSwag exports at exactly the right specs.

    Photo Requirements in Detail

    Face and Expression

    Face clearly visible and centered. Neutral expression, both eyes open, mouth closed. Full head visible from top of hair to chin. Both ears should be visible.

    Background

    Plain white or off-white. No patterns, shadows, or other people. Stand 4 feet from a white wall with even lighting.

    Glasses

    No glasses of any kind since November 2016. This applies to all US visa types.

    What to Wear

    Normal street clothes. Avoid white clothing that blends with the background. No uniforms (except religious). No hats or head coverings (religious exceptions apply).

    Common Rejection Reasons

    1. Wrong pixel dimensions — Must be 600×600 to 1200×1200 for DS-160
    2. Wrong file format — Must be JPEG, not PNG or HEIC
    3. File too large or small — Must be 54KB to 10MB
    4. Shadows — On face or background
    5. Glasses — Not allowed
    6. Non-white background — Must be white
    7. Photo too old — Must be within 6 months
    8. Head size wrong — Must be 50–69% of image height
