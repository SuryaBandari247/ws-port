    🇺🇸 United States — Green Card Photo

    The US Green Card (Permanent Resident Card) is one of the most sought-after immigration documents in the world. Whether you're applying through family sponsorship (I-130), employment (I-140/I-485), or the Diversity Visa Lottery (DV), you'll need passport-style photos.

    US Green Card Photo Specs

    SpecificationRequirement
    Photo size2 × 2 inches (51 × 51 mm)
    Head height (chin to crown)1 – 1⅜ inches (25–35 mm)
    BackgroundPlain white
    ExpressionNeutral, mouth closed
    GlassesNot allowed
    RecencyWithin 30 days of filing (I-485) or 6 months (DV Lottery)
    Quantity2 identical photos

    Application-Specific Requirements

    • Form I-485 (Adjustment of Status) — 2 photos, taken within 30 days
    • Form I-751 (Remove Conditions) — 2 photos
    • DV Lottery — Digital photo: 600×600 pixels, JPEG, max 240 KB
    • Consular Processing (DS-260) — 2 photos at interview
    DV Lottery note: The Diversity Visa Lottery requires a digital photo uploaded online. It must be exactly 600×600 pixels in JPEG format, between 54 KB and 240 KB. WithSwag can export at these exact specifications.
    Official Source: USCIS — Photo requirements | State Department photo specs

    🇨🇦 Canada — PR Card Photo

    Canada's Permanent Resident Card requires photos that meet the same specifications as Canadian passport photos. The PR card is renewed every 5 years and requires new photos each time.

    Canada PR Card Photo Specs

    SpecificationRequirement
    Photo size50 × 70 mm
    Face height (chin to crown)31–36 mm
    BackgroundPlain white or light-coloured
    ExpressionNeutral, mouth closed
    GlassesNot allowed
    RecencyWithin the last 12 months
    Quantity2 identical

    Canadian PR Photo Rules

    • Photos must be taken by a commercial photographer
    • Photographer's name, address, and date must be on the back of one photo
    • Your name and date of birth on the back of the other photo
    • Both colour and black-and-white are accepted
    • Must show full front view of face and shoulders
    Official Source: Government of Canada (IRCC) — PR Card application | Photo specifications

    🇬🇧 United Kingdom — ILR / Settlement Photo

    UK Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR), also known as settlement, grants permanent residence in the UK. The photo requirements are identical to UK passport photos.

    UK ILR Photo Specs

    SpecificationRequirement
    Photo size35 × 45 mm
    Head height (chin to crown)29–34 mm
    BackgroundPlain light grey or cream
    ExpressionNeutral, mouth closed
    GlassesNot allowed
    RecencyWithin the last month
    Quantity2

    ILR Application Routes

    • 5-year route — Work visa holders (Skilled Worker, Health & Care Worker)
    • 10-year route — Long residence
    • Spouse/partner route — After 5 years on family visa
    • EU Settlement Scheme — Pre-settled to settled status
    Official Source: GOV.UK — Indefinite Leave to Remain | Photo requirements

    🇩🇪 Germany — Niederlassungserlaubnis Photo

    The German permanent residence permit (Niederlassungserlaubnis) is issued after holding a temporary residence permit for a qualifying period (typically 5 years, or 21–33 months with an EU Blue Card). The photo must be biometric.

    Common Rejection Reasons for PR Photos

    1. Photo too old — Must be recent (30 days to 12 months depending on country)
    2. Wrong background — Must match country requirements exactly
    3. Glasses worn — Most countries now prohibit glasses
    4. Missing photographer stamp — Required in Canada
    5. Missing countersignature — Required in UK and Australia
    6. Wrong dimensions — Must be exact size for your country
    7. Low resolution — Must be sharp and clear, especially for biometric
    8. Shadows or uneven lighting — Must be evenly lit
