Country-Specific Variations

While the EU has a standard 35×45mm format, individual countries have slight variations:

Germany

Strict biometric requirements. Background must be light grey (not white). Head must be centered with very precise positioning. Glasses are strongly discouraged.

France

Accepts light grey or light blue background. The face must occupy 70–80% of the photo. Expression must be completely neutral.

Netherlands

Light grey background required. Both ears must be visible. No hair covering the face. Very strict on head positioning.

Italy

White or light background accepted. Standard 35×45mm. Recent photo within 6 months.

Spain

White background required. 32×26mm face size within the 35×45mm frame. Recent photo.

Schengen Visa Photo Requirements

Schengen visa photos follow the same 35×45mm standard. The photo must be biometric-compliant, meaning it meets ICAO (International Civil Aviation Organization) standards for facial recognition systems.

Tip: If you're applying for a Schengen visa, the photo requirements are the same as the passport photo for the country you're applying through. Use WithSwag to select the specific country for exact specifications.
