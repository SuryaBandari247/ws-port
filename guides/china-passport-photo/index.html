Chinese Visa Photo Requirements

If you're applying for a Chinese visa (tourist, business, student), the photo requirements are the same 33×48mm format with white background. Some consulates may also accept 2×2 inch (51×51mm) photos, but 33×48mm is the standard.

Key Requirements

  • White background only — No other colors accepted
  • Front-facing — Face must be directly facing the camera
  • Both ears visible — Hair must not cover ears
  • No head coverings — Unless for religious reasons
  • No heavy makeup — Natural appearance required
  • Recent photo — Taken within 6 months
Digital submission: For online Chinese visa applications, the photo must be exactly 354×472 pixels, between 40–120 KB, in JPEG format. WithSwag can export at these exact specifications.
