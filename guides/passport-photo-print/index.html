    What You Need

    • A passport photo created with WithSwag
    • Glossy photo paper (4×6 inch or A4 size)
    • An inkjet or laser printer, OR access to a drugstore photo kiosk

    Step-by-Step: Print at Home

    1. Create your passport photo at withswag.org/portrait
    2. Choose "Photo Collage" mode instead of single photo
    3. Select your paper size (4×6, A4, or 5×7)
    4. Set the number of photos you want on the sheet
    5. Download the collage as a high-resolution PNG or JPG (300 DPI)
    6. Print on glossy photo paper at "Best Quality" or "Photo" setting
    7. Cut along the grid lines with scissors or a paper cutter

    Print at a Drugstore (Cheapest Option)

    If you don't have a photo printer, the cheapest option is to download the collage from WithSwag and print it at a drugstore photo kiosk:

    1. Download the 4×6 collage from WithSwag
    2. Transfer to your phone or USB drive
    3. Go to any CVS, Walgreens, Walmart, or Target photo kiosk
    4. Print as a standard 4×6 photo (~$0.35)
    5. Cut the individual photos apart
    Result: You get 4+ passport photos for about $3.35 total instead of $16.99 for just 2 photos at the same store.

    Printing Tips for Best Quality

    • Use glossy or semi-glossy photo paper — matte paper may not be accepted
    • Set printer to "Best Quality" or "Photo" mode
    • Make sure the print is at actual size (100%) — don't let the printer scale it
    • Let the print dry completely before cutting
    • Use a paper cutter or ruler + craft knife for clean edges
