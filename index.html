Reduce Photo Size to 200KB, 240KB, or 500KB

One of the most common problems during visa and passport applications is file size rejection. The DS-160 US visa form requires photos under 240KB. Indian government portals like Aadhaar and PAN card uploads often need photos under 200KB. Schengen visa portals cap uploads at 500KB. Our compress tool uses binary search compression to hit your exact target size while preserving maximum image quality. Different countries require different passport photo pixel dimensions. US passport and DS-160 visa photos need 600×600 pixels. Schengen and EU biometric photos need 413×531 pixels. Canada PR photos need 420×540 pixels. China passport photos need 390×567 pixels.
