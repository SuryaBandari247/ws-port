Create a travel itinerary for your visa application. Enter your flight details and get a formatted document you can print.

⚠️ UK Transit Warning for Asian Passport Holders: If you hold a passport from India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Philippines, China, or most other Asian countries, do not book flights transiting through London (Heathrow, Gatwick) unless you have a UK Direct Airside Transit Visa (DATV). A Schengen visa does NOT allow UK transit. Instead, route through Amsterdam (AMS), Frankfurt (FRA), Paris (CDG), or Istanbul (IST). Being denied boarding costs you the flight and can jeopardize your visa application.
