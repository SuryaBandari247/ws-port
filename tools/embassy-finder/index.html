💡 How to Find Your Specific Embassy

Not sure which office handles your visa? Follow these steps:

  • For Schengen visas: Apply at the embassy of the country where you'll spend the most time. If equal, apply at the country of first entry.
  • For US visas: Go to ustraveldocs.com, select your country, and book an appointment.
  • For UK visas: Use the GOV.UK visa centre finder.
  • For any country: Search "[country] embassy in [your city]" on Google.
